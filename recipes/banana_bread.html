Banana Bread

Description

How to make a beautifully easy and comforting dish at any level!

Ingredients

  • Very ripe bananas.
  • Butter
  • Self raising flour
  • Eggs
  • Caster sugar
  • baking powder

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 180C.
  2. Grease the loaf tin.
  3. Combine dry ingredients with butter and eggs until fluffy.
  4. Add your bananas.
  5. Pour the mixture into the tin and bake for 50 minutes.
  6. Enjoy your delicious banana bread!
