Lasagna

Description

A beautiful tasting Lasagna in no time at all! Lets go over the ingredients needed below:

  • Minced Beef
  • Pasta Sheets
  • Garlic
  • Cheese
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Brown the mince and onions in a pan.
  2. In a seperate pan make the cheese sauce.
  3. Combine the tomatoes with the beef, onions and garlic.
  4. In a lasagna dish build up layers of beef sauce, pasta and cheese sauce.
  5. Top it off with some more cheese and put it in the Oven to bake for an hour.
  6. Enjoy your delicious Lasagna!
