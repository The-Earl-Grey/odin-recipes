Apple Pie

Description

A beautiful tasting Apple Pie in no time at all!

Ingredients

  • Apples
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Honey
  • Egg
  • Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 200C
  2. Sift the flour into a bowl, add butter and mix with fingers.
  3. Add 3 tbsp cold water, wrap in cling film and place into the fridge to chill.
  4. Slice the apples add to pie dish with honey and cinnamon.
  5. Roll out the pastry, lay it over the pie dish and press the edges onto the dish.
  6. Make a small cut to let air escape and bake in the oven for 30 mins.
  7. Enjoy!
